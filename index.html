About Me

I’m a passionate Full-Stack Developer with 3+ years of hands-on experience designing, developing, and deploying modern web applications using Angular, Java (Spring Boot), and Microservices architecture.

I specialize in building scalable, high-performance SPAs with Angular (6 – 17), leveraging lazy loading, RxJS, and state management using NgRx/Redux. On the backend, I have strong expertise in Java, RESTful APIs, and event-driven microservices with Spring Boot, Spring Data JPA, and Hibernate.

Beyond coding, I thrive in Agile environments, actively contributing to sprint planning, code reviews, and cross-functional collaboration with UI/UX, QA, and DevOps teams. I follow SOLID principles, design patterns, and test-driven development practices to deliver clean, maintainable, and production-ready solutions.
